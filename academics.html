Bope Rajasinghe National School

Bope Rajasinghe School is a prominent Buddhist school, located in Padukka area in the Colombo district, Western Province. It's a secondary school with classes ranging from grade 6 - 13. The student body is nearly 1700. with an academic staff of 67. With a proud history of 146 years, our school started with 4 students under the name of 'Swabhasha Siphala' and was registered as a government school in 1872. At present it is fully developed as a 1 ABS school with all the facilities required by Science,Maths, Art, Commerce and Technology streams.
